# Anwar Burka Shop MVP - 3 Minute Demo

Open: http://localhost:4173

## Show This Flow

1. Start on **Home** and show the premium reel-first landing experience.
2. Open **Collections** to show category banners and product carousels.
3. Open **Offers** to show campaign blocks and video-style promo tiles.
4. Go to **Shop Reels** and scroll the vertical product reels.
5. Click **Order** on any product.
6. Select size, enter customer details, and click **Place Order**.
7. The dashboard jumps to **Orders** and shows the new order.
8. Go to **Owner Panel** and publish a sample product to show how the shop owner can add new reel products.

## Client Pitch Line

This MVP turns your existing product reels into a simple digital shop. Customers watch, tap, and order. The shop owner manages products and orders without paying for a mobile app, payment gateway, or logistics system in phase 1.
